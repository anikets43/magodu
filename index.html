ABOUT TRUST

Nanjamma Charitable Trust (NCT) focuses on economic and social empowerment of all children in "Magodu village" present in Sira taluk, Tumkur district, Karnataka, India Our goal is to equip all children of "Magodu" to go beyond basic literacy and rudimentary skills. We plan to equip them with specified knowledge, skills and values to enable them achieve excellence in whatever they do while also becoming good human beings by being productive, self-reliant and socially responsible citizens. Through this effort, the trust is committed to raising the level of literacy and to help disadvantaged children realise their full potential in "Magodu".

"Magodu" currently has around 150 children studying in primary and secondary schools (1st to 7th grade). However, most of them would not attend high school, since they would not be able to afford high school education expenses. Some students are able to attend high school, but discontinue at either 10th or 12th grade due to the same issues. A very small percentage of kids who are able to attend professional colleges (Engineering/Medical etc) struggle for their education and living expenses trying to get a quality education
