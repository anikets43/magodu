ABOUT US

Nanjamma & Sons Charitable trust (NSCT), founded by Nanjamma and her sons (Shivakumar & Balakrishna), is a non-profit NGO dedicated towards empowerment of Magodu children. NSCT’s vision is to empower Magodu children to help them build a strong foundation for their better future. The mission of this trust is to equip children of the Magodu village to go beyond literacy and rudimentary skills with specified knowledge, skills and values to enable them to become good human beings and productive, socially responsible citizens and to achieve excellence in whatever they do. his effort is specifically focused on children in Magodu village, Sira Taluk, Tumkur district, Karnataka, India. We give special focus on girls and underprivileged Magodu children who cannot otherwise access or afford the quality education NSCT opens the door for rural children to successfully pursue higher studies and gainful employment. The holistic, activity-based approach nurtures children’s development beyond just academics, ensuring that students realize their full potential and learn in a joyful manner. With access to quality education, youth in Magodu can be empowered to take care of their own and the country’s development.

CURRENT PROBLEM

Magodu is a small village is located in Sira Tehsil of Tumkur district in Karnataka, India. It is situated 10km away from sub-district headquarter Sira and 60km away from district headquarter Tumkur. As per 2009 stats, Maagodu is the gram panchayat of Magodu village. The total geographical area of village is 1870.81 hectares. Magodu has a total population of around 3000 people and there are about 650 houses. Sira is nearest town to Magodu which is approximately 10km away. Magodu has suffered a lot last 15+ years due to lack of rain and completely dried out Due to which, many families have become extremely poor which inturn not able to afford basic education. Magodu has a small Govt. school which runs classes from nursery to 7th grade and around 150 kids studying in this school. For further education, kids need to goto near by big village or Sira town. Many kids stop their education at 7th grade for many reasons including but not limited to “lack of financial capability”, “lack of encouragement & motivation”, “lack of interest in learning and thinking higher” etc. Parents are not able to send their kids for further studies or better education likeNavodaya or higher education etc. just because they can’t afford the fees. Due to lack of quality education, culture and healthy environment for high thinking at both academic and other activities like sports etc, the children are not able to compete with urban children. As a result, these children are not able to see better future and stuck in the village going to work in fields. We @ NSCT, are trying to bridge this gap.


"EVERY MAGODU CHILD DESERVES A BETTER FUTURE"

We @NSCT believe that lack of literacy is the root cause of many major problems in the country. We also believe that it is better to take care of our own places / areas within our reach first before solving the global problems. So, we selected our own village (Magodu) for the same purpose. Magodu is where we all were born and spent most of our childhood days.

  • To make children follow healthy lifestyle to be better citizen
  • To make children adopt social responsibility in their lifestyle

Shivakumar Chandrashekar
Founder
I was born in Magodu village on 18th January. I used to spend all my summer vacations in this village during my childhood. I was fortunate enough to be graduated and then work in both India and USA but my old friends in Magodu village could not continue their education for many reasons and mainly because of lack of encouragement, motivation, financial support and resources. After coming back from USA in 2014, I visited this village and felt bad seeing many kids are not getting the support they need for better future. So, decided this initiative to help whatever possible to these children and their better future along with wonderful support from my family and friends. I am currently working in Bangalore as a Director in a large global bank.
