Overall objectives:

  • To make children aware of global knowledge in all areas to be smart and competitive with urban children
  • To make children fluent in English and adept excellent communication skills
  • To make children adept in computer skills as well as awareness of latest technology
  • To make children follow healthy lifestyle to be better citizen
  • To make children adopt social responsibility in their lifestyle

How do we achieve above objectives ?
Below are some of the means (but not limited to) by which we are planning to achieve the above objectives:

  • By providing necessary resources required
  • By creating supportive culture & environment
  • By encouraging, motivating, trainings, mentoring & coaching
  • By introducing train-the-trainer model (which includes school
  • By providing financial support for the disadvantaged children

What activities we do to meet the above objectives?
Below are some of the activities (but not limited to) by which we are planning to achieve the above objectives:

  • Provide regular health camps, dental, physical, eye etc..

  • Bringing specially abled/ physical handicapped into mainstream education Giving a library and empowering the children regarding importance of self- paced learning
  • Equipping the children emotionally to handle their grievances through personal counselling / group counselling Entrepreneurship training, providing a platform to showcase their entrepreneurial skills
  • Providing basic amenities in the schools like rest rooms for boys and girls separately and empowering children to maintain environmental cleanliness
